# Official Rollout Plan: Coffee Personality Quiz
**Basecamp Coffee — All 45 Locations**
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Overview

The Coffee Personality Quiz is moving from pilot to permanent program feature. This document covers how we roll it out consistently across all 45 locations in Seattle, Portland, and Boise.

---

## Timeline

**Week 1 — Materials & Training**
- Print QR code cards for all 45 locations (use cc4e-coffeecourse.vercel.app/print)
- Distribute barista training doc to all store managers
- Brief store managers on the quiz, personality types, and counter experience

**Week 2 — In-Store Launch**
- QR code cards live at every counter
- Baristas trained and ready
- Member announcement email sent

**Week 3 — Monitor & Support**
- Check in with store managers on barista questions
- Monitor quiz completion rate for any drop-off
- Address any issues quickly

---

## What Each Store Needs

**Physical:**
- 2x printed QR code cards per location (counter + window)
- Print from: cc4e-coffeecourse.vercel.app/print

**Digital:**
- Barista reference page bookmarked on counter tablet: cc4e-coffeecourse.vercel.app/barista
- Store manager briefed on personality types

**Training:**
- Barista training doc (see barista-training.md)
- 15-minute team huddle before launch week

---

## Store Manager Checklist

- [ ] Received and read barista training doc
- [ ] Held team huddle with baristas
- [ ] QR code cards printed and placed at counter
- [ ] Barista reference page bookmarked on tablet
- [ ] Confirmed baristas can name all 4 personality types

---

## Locations

**Seattle** — 22 locations
**Portland** — 15 locations
**Boise** — 8 locations

Regional managers responsible for confirming checklist completion by end of Week 1.
